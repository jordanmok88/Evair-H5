iOS

06

How to switch carriers on iOS devices

Use this if your iPhone or iPad does not automatically connect to a good network.

1Insert SIM card

Insert the EvairSIM card and wait for the device to detect it.

2Open Settings

Tap Cellular, then tap the SIM card that uses EvairSIM.

3Enable Data Roaming

Turn on Data Roaming and tap Network Selection.

4Select carrier manually

Disable Automatic, wait 1-2 minutes, then manually select the carrier with the strongest signal.

iOS APN

07

How to configure APN for iOS devices

Most iOS devices connect automatically. If not, set APN manually.

1Insert SIM card

Install the SIM and wait for the phone to recognize it.

2Open Cellular

Open Settings, tap Cellular, then select the EvairSIM SIM card.

3Open data network

Enable Data Roaming, then tap Cellular Data Network.

4Set APN

Enter APN plus. Leave Username and Password blank, then save.

Android

08

How to switch carriers on Android devices

Android menu names vary by brand, but the network selection flow is similar.

1Insert SIM card

Insert the EvairSIM card and power on your device.

2Open Settings

Tap Mobile Network, then tap SIM Card.

3Enable roaming

Turn on Data Roaming and tap Carrier or Network Operator.

4Select manually

Disable Automatic Selection, wait 1-2 minutes, then manually choose a carrier.

Android APN

09

How to configure APN for Android devices

Use this if your device has no internet after data roaming is enabled.

1Open APN settings

Go to Settings, Network or Mobile Network, then APN or Access Point Names.

2Add a new APN

Tap +, Add New APN, or Manual Configuration.

3Enter APN details

Set APN to plus. Leave Username and Password blank.

4Save and select

Save the APN, select it as active, and wait for the connection.

MiFi

10

How to set up APN on MiFi

For mobile Wi-Fi, hotspots, and routers, enter the APN from the admin panel.

1Insert SIM card

Insert the SIM card into the MiFi, hotspot, or router.

2Access admin panel

Open Chrome, Safari, or another browser. Enter the Wi-Fi hotspot or router IP address.

3Common IP addresses

The IP is often 192.168.0.1 or 192.168.1.1. Some devices print it on the rear label.

4Log in

Use your admin username and password. Check your device manual if needed.

Camera setup

12

What if a Reolink camera fails to connect automatically?

Based on Reolink camera settings. Some cameras require APN import by Micro SD card.

1Create a configuration file

On a computer, create a TXT file named my_custom_apn.

2Add APN information

Enter apn=plus, username=, and password=. Leave username and password empty.

3Transfer to SD card

Save my_custom_apn.txt to the root directory of a FAT32 Micro SD card.

4Import to camera

Turn off the camera, then install the SIM card and Micro SD card.

Q&A

15
1. Why is there no network connection after inserting the SIM card?

Please enable Data Roaming on your phone and wait about 2 minutes for the SIM card to activate and connect automatically. Alternatively, restart your phone after enabling roaming to restore the network connection.

2. Why is there still no network connection after enabling roaming?

Your device may not support automatic APN configuration. Please manually set the APN to one of the following: globaldata, plus, or internet.

Q&A

16
3. Why does my SIM card show as expired even though I have not used all the data?

EvairSIM SIM cards support automatic APN configuration. Some brands of mobile Wi-Fi and other IoT devices have roaming enabled by default, which triggers automatic activation once the SIM card is inserted. The plan will show as expired once the package duration ends.

4. Why does my device display Invalid SIM, SIM Locked, No Service, or SIM Network Unlock PIN?

This indicates that your device is carrier-locked and does not support SIM cards from other providers. Contact your device provider to unlock it. EvairSIM IoT cards are compatible with unlocked devices.

Q&A

17
5. Why is there still no internet after I enabled data roaming and configured APN on my mobile Wi-Fi?

Please check if your device supports US network bands. Our SIM cards support these 4G/LTE bands: B2, B4, B12, B13, B14, B30, B66, and B71.

For 5G, supported bands include n5, n41, n71, n77, n258, n260, and n261.

If your device supports these bands

Take screenshots of your Data Roaming and APN configuration pages and send them to EvairSIM support if it still cannot connect.

Q&A

18
6. How can I contact you?

You can reach us via the app or by emailing service@evairdigital.com.

7. Why is my network speed only 50 Mbps?

Your current network speed depends on the carrier your device selects and the signal strength in your area. Try manually switching network providers and choosing the strongest signal among AT&T, Verizon, or T-Mobile.

8. Why did my internet stop working after I had been using it for a while?

Log in to the EvairSIM app or website and wait 15 minutes to check if your data package has been exhausted.

Before changing APN again

First check your remaining data and signal. If you still have data, try manually switching carriers before creating a new APN profile.

Q&A

19
What if I still have data but cannot connect?

If you still have data but cannot connect, we recommend manually switching to a different carrier.

9. Do I need to buy a new SIM card once my data is used up?

No. Download the EvairSIM app or visit the top-up page to subscribe to a new plan. You can continue using the same SIM card.

10. Why is my security camera unable to connect after inserting the SIM card?

Your device may not support automatic APN configuration. Contact the camera support team to ask how to manually configure APN. The EvairSIM APN is globaldata, plus, or internet.

For cameras

If the camera brand has no visible APN menu, use the camera maker's support instructions or the SD-card import method shown earlier in this booklet.

Q&A

20
11. Can the US SIM card I purchased be used in Mexico or Europe?

No. If you need to use our SIM card in multiple regions, such as the US and Europe, or the US, Mexico, and Canada, you will need to purchase our Multi-Region SIM card.

12. Does the SIM card provide a static US IP address?

Not exactly. The EvairSIM IoT card provides a local US IP, which supports US-based apps and streaming services. Because the card supports all three major US carriers, the IP address may change during network fluctuations or when switching between carriers.

Q&A

21
13. Can I use the SIM card in American Samoa?

No. The EvairSIM card is supported in the Mainland US, Alaska, and Hawaii. It is currently not supported in other US overseas territories or islands.
