Camera setup

16

Reolink camera APN setup

Some cameras require APN import by SD card.

1Create a TXT file

On a computer, create a text file named my_custom_apn.

2Add APN information

Enter apn=plus, username=, and password=. Leave username and password empty.

Camera setup

17

Reolink camera APN setup

3Transfer to SD card

Save my_custom_apn.txt to the root directory of a FAT32 Micro SD card.

4Import to camera

Turn off the camera. Install the SIM and SD card. Power on, then press reset for a few seconds.

Q&A

19
1. Why is there no network connection after inserting the SIM card?

Enable Data Roaming and wait about 2 minutes for the SIM card to activate and connect automatically. You can also restart your phone after enabling roaming.

2. Why is there still no network connection after enabling roaming?

Your device may not support automatic APN configuration. Manually set the APN to globaldata, plus, or internet.

Q&A

20
3. Why does my SIM show as expired even though I have not used all the data?

Some MiFi and IoT devices have roaming enabled by default. This can activate the SIM when it is inserted. The plan shows expired once the package duration ends.

4. Why does my device display Invalid SIM, SIM Locked, No Service, or SIM Network Unlock PIN?

Your device is likely carrier-locked. Contact your device provider to unlock it. EvairSIM IoT cards work with unlocked devices.

Q&A

21
5. Why is there still no internet after roaming and APN setup on my mobile Wi-Fi?

Please check if your device supports US network bands. Our SIM cards support B2, B4, B12, B13, B14, B30, B66, B71 for 4G/LTE and n5, n41, n71, n77, n258, n260, n261 for 5G.

If your device supports these bands but still cannot connect, send screenshots of your roaming and APN pages to EvairSIM support.

Q&A

22
6. How can I contact you?

You can reach us via the app or by emailing service@evairdigital.com.

7. Why is my network speed only 50 Mbps?

Speed depends on the selected carrier and local signal strength. Try manually switching between AT&T, Verizon, or T-Mobile and choose the strongest signal.

Q&A

23
8. Why did my internet stop working after I had been using it for a while?

Log in to the EvairSIM app or website and wait 15 minutes to check if your data package has been exhausted. If you still have data, manually switch to a different carrier.

9. Do I need to buy a new SIM card once my data is used up?

No. Use the EvairSIM app or top-up page to subscribe to a new plan. You can continue using the same SIM card.

Q&A

24
10. Why is my security camera unable to connect after inserting the SIM card?

Your device may not support automatic APN configuration. Contact the camera support team to ask how to manually configure APN. The EvairSIM APN is globaldata, plus, or internet.

11. Can the US SIM card be used in Mexico or Europe?

No. For multiple regions, such as the US and Europe or the US, Mexico, and Canada, purchase our Multi-Region SIM card.

Q&A

25
12. Does the SIM card provide a static US IP address?

Not exactly. The EvairSIM IoT card provides a local US IP, which supports US-based apps and streaming services.

Because the card supports all three major US carriers, the IP address may change during network fluctuations or carrier switching.

Q&A

26
13. Can I use the SIM card in American Samoa?

No. The EvairSIM card is supported in the Mainland US, Alaska, and Hawaii. It is not currently supported in other US overseas territories or islands.
